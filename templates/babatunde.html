Rev. Moses Babatunde

DCC Chairman

Rev. Moses Ayando Babatunde serves as the DCC Chairman for ECWA Lagos West, providing visionary leadership and spiritual guidance to the district. His leadership has been pivotal in organizing and sustaining impactful events like the ECWA Glory Concert.

With a deep commitment to community building and spiritual growth, Rev. Ayando has overseen numerous initiatives that strengthen the church's presence and influence in the region. His pastoral care and administrative acumen have earned him respect among peers and congregants alike.

As the DCC Chairman, Rev. Ayando plays a key role in ensuring that the Glory Concert remains a beacon of worship and unity. His dedication to God's work continues to inspire many in their faith journey.
