Rev. Dr. Frank Oluwatimilehin Paul

Guest Minister

Rev. Dr. Frank Olutayo Omowole is a distinguished theologian and guest minister known for his profound teachings and spiritual leadership. As a respected figure in the Christian community, he brings years of experience in ministry and biblical scholarship.

His ministry focuses on deep biblical exposition, spiritual growth, and community building. Rev. Dr. Omowole has served in various leadership roles within the church and has been instrumental in mentoring young ministers and scholars.

As a guest minister at the ECWA Glory Concert, Rev. Dr. Omowole brings his wealth of knowledge and passion for God's word, inspiring attendees with messages of hope, faith, and spiritual renewal.
