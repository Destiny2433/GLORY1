Min. Pelumi Deborah

Gospel Minister

Min. Pelumi Deborah is a Spirit-filled gospel minister and worship leader known for her deep, soulful worship and powerful vocal ministry. She has a unique calling to lead people into an intimate encounter with God through music.

With a heart for service and a passion for the gospel, Pelumi has been a blessing to many congregations. Her ministry is characterized by a strong presence of the Holy Spirit and a commitment to authentic worship that transcends boundaries.

As a featured minister at the ECWA Glory Concert, Min. Pelumi Deborah brings a fresh sound of worship that inspires and uplifts. Her ability to lead the congregation into profound moments of praise makes her a vital part of this year's concert experience.
