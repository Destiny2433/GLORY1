About the ECWA Glory Concert

'Ecwa Glory Concert' is a free, all-night Gospel music concert featuring some of the most celebrated and exceptional psalmists from Nigeria and around the world. This phenomenal concert is convened annually by Ecwa Lagos West DCC (LWDCC). Birthed by a God-inspired vision, 'Ecwa Glory Concert' provides a platform where all can raise their hearts, hands and voices together as one in unfettered praise to the Almighty irrespective of tribe, denomination, creed or language.

The concert has grown from humble beginnings to become one of Nigeria's premier gospel music events, attracting thousands of worshippers each year. It serves as a unifying force, bringing together people from diverse backgrounds in a celebration of faith, music, and community.

Over the years, the ECWA Glory Concert has featured renowned gospel artists and ministers, creating unforgettable moments of worship and spiritual renewal. The event continues to inspire and uplift souls, fostering a deeper connection with God through music and fellowship.

Our History

The ECWA Glory Concert was established in year 1999 with a divine vision to create a space where worship transcends boundaries. Founded by the ECWA Lagos West District Church Council, the concert has become a beacon of hope and praise in the Nigerian gospel music scene.

Each year, the concert evolves, incorporating new elements while maintaining its core mission of glorifying God through music. From intimate gatherings to massive celebrations, the event has touched countless lives and continues to grow in impact and reach.

Our Mission

To provide a platform for unified worship, bringing together believers from all walks of life to celebrate God's goodness through music and fellowship. The ECWA Glory Concert aims to:

  • Foster spiritual growth and renewal
  • Promote unity among diverse Christian communities
  • Showcase exceptional gospel talent
  • Create memorable experiences of worship and praise
  • Spread the message of hope and faith through music
