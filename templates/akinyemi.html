Rev. Francis Arowolo

DCC Secretary

Rev. Francis Segun Arowolo serves as the DCC Secretary for ECWA Lagos West DCC, bringing extensive administrative experience and spiritual leadership to the Glory Concert organization.

As a dedicated servant of God, Rev. Arowolo plays a crucial role in coordinating the various aspects of the concert, ensuring smooth operations and effective communication between all stakeholders.

His commitment to excellence and attention to detail have been instrumental in the success of previous Glory Concert events, making him an invaluable member of the organizing committee.
